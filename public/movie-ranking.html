The Best Movies According to Kasun

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Mickey 17

Directed by Bong Joon Ho, this sci-fi thriller follows Mickey, an "expendable" who embarks on a perilous mission to colonize an ice planet. The film stars Robert Pattinson, Steven Yeun, and Michael Monroe. With its gripping storyline and stunning visuals, "Mickey 17" has captivated audiences and critics alike.

The Gorge

This action-packed film, directed by Scott Derrickson, features Miles Teller and Anya Taylor-Joy as two highly-trained operatives sent to protect opposite sides of a mysterious gorge. As an evil force emerges, they must work together to survive. The movie's intense action sequences and compelling character dynamics have made it a must-watch.

Captain America: Brave New World

Directed by Julius Onah, this superhero film stars Anthony Mackie as Sam Wilson, the new Captain America. The story revolves around an international incident that forces Sam to uncover a nefarious global plan. With its thrilling plot and powerful performances, "Captain America: Brave New World" has become a fan favorite.
